About ACRY

ACRY is dedicated to helping archers reach their full potential. Through the combined expertise of our partners and sophisticated AI models, we provide insights and guidance tailored to each individual archer's performance and characteristics. Our mission is rooted in accessibility and advancement, ensuring that archers from all backgrounds have the resources they need to excel.

How Our Program Works

Our innovative approach utilizes a combination of your archery scores and responses from our tailored Google Docs. Based on this data, our AI model delivers insightful feedback on your performance and provides comprehensive visuals that detail the analysis of your scores and their patterns.
